# NikahBook - Child Safety Standards

**Last Updated**: January 2025  
**Contact**: support@nikahbook.com

## Overview

NikahBook is a Muslim matrimonial application designed exclusively for adults aged 18 years and older. We maintain a zero-tolerance policy toward child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) and child sexual abuse material (CSAM).

## Age Restrictions

### Minimum Age Requirement
- **Minimum Age**: 18 years old
- **Verification Method**: Age gate during registration
- Users must declare their date of birth during profile creation
- Profiles indicating age below 18 are automatically rejected
- No exceptions are made to this policy

### Age Verification Process
1. Users enter date of birth during registration
2. System calculates age automatically
3. Registration is blocked if age < 18 years
4. Profile creation cannot proceed without valid age verification

## Child Safety Measures

### Prevention
- **Adult-Only Platform**: No users under 18 permitted
- **Profile Screening**: All profiles reviewed for age compliance
- **Automated Detection**: System flags suspicious age-related content
- **Manual Review**: Admin verification of all new profiles

### Detection
- **Content Monitoring**: All uploaded photos screened
- **Behavior Analysis**: Suspicious activity flagged for review
- **User Reports**: In-app reporting system for safety concerns
- **Automated Scanning**: AI-assisted detection of inappropriate content

### Response Protocol
1. **Immediate Action**: Suspicious accounts suspended instantly
2. **Investigation**: Dedicated team reviews flagged content within 24 hours
3. **Law Enforcement**: CSAM reports forwarded to authorities immediately
4. **Permanent Ban**: Zero-tolerance enforcement with no appeals
