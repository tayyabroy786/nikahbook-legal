# Data Deletion Request - NikahBook

## How to Request Data Deletion

NikahBook respects your right to control your personal data. You can request deletion of some or all of your data without deleting your account.

## Steps to Request Data Deletion

### Option 1: In-App Request (Recommended)
1. Open NikahBook app
2. Go to **Profile** → **Settings**
3. Tap **Privacy & Data**
4. Select **Request Data Deletion**
5. Choose what data to delete
6. Submit request

### Option 2: Email Request
Send an email to **support@nikahbook.com** with:
- **Subject**: Data Deletion Request
- **Email**: Your registered email address
- **Request Type**: Specify what data you want deleted (see below)

## What Data Can Be Deleted

### Partial Deletion (Account Remains Active)
You can request deletion of:
- ✅ **Photos**: All or specific profile photos
- ✅ **Chat History**: All messages and conversations
- ✅ **Match History**: Past match requests and connections
- ✅ **Search History**: Your search and filter history

### Complete Deletion (Account Deleted)
Request deletion of:
- ✅ **All Profile Data**: Name, age, personal information
- ✅ **All Photos**: Profile pictures and uploads
- ✅ **All Messages**: Chat conversations
- ✅ **All Matches**: Match requests and connections
- ✅ **Account**: Complete account removal

## Data Deletion Timeline

| Data Type | Deletion Time | Notes |
|-----------|---------------|-------|
| Photos | Immediate | Removed from storage within 24 hours |
| Chat Messages | Immediate | Deleted from both users' views |
| Profile Data | 24-48 hours | Removed from active database |
| Account | 30 days | Grace period for account recovery |
| Backups | 90 days | Removed from backup systems |

## Data That May Be Retained

For legal and security purposes, we may retain:
- ❌ **Transaction Records**: For 7 years (legal requirement)
- ❌ **Abuse Reports**: If your account was reported
- ❌ **Legal Holds**: Data subject to legal proceedings
- ❌ **Anonymized Analytics**: Non-identifiable usage data

## Verification Process

To protect your privacy, we verify all deletion requests:
1. We send a verification email to your registered email
2. Click the verification link within 48 hours
3. Confirm your deletion request
4. We process your request within the specified timeline

## After Deletion

Once data is deleted:
- ✅ Data is permanently removed and cannot be recovered
- ✅ You will receive confirmation email
- ✅ For partial deletion, your account remains active
- ✅ For complete deletion, you can create a new account anytime

## Contact Us

For data deletion requests or questions:

**Email**: support@nikahbook.com  
**Subject**: Data Deletion Request  
**Response Time**: Within 48 hours

Include in your email:
- Your registered email address
- Type of data you want deleted
- Reason for deletion (optional)

## Your Rights

Under data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA), you have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your data
- Export your data
- Restrict processing
- Object to processing

## Important Notes

- Data deletion is **permanent and irreversible**
- Deleted data cannot be recovered
- Some data may be retained for legal compliance
- Account deletion removes all app access
- You can create a new account after deletion
