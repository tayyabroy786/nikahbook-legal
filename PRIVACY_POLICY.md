# Privacy Policy for NikahBook

**Last Updated: January 2025**

## Introduction

NikahBook ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Muslim matrimonial mobile application (the "App").

By using NikahBook, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Information You Provide

When you create an account and use NikahBook, we collect:

- **Contact Information**: Phone number (for OTP authentication)
- **Profile Information**: Name, age, date of birth, gender, height, weight, marital status
- **Religious Information**: Sect, cast, prayer habits, religious practices
- **Educational Information**: Education level, occupation, income
- **Family Information**: Family background, family values
- **Location Information**: City, country, current location
- **Photos**: Profile pictures and additional photos (up to 6)
- **Preferences**: Partner preferences including age range, height, sect, cast, education, location
- **Communication Data**: Messages sent through our in-app chat system

### 1.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App features used, time spent on app, interaction patterns
- **Log Data**: IP address, access times, app crashes, performance data
- **Analytics Data**: User behavior, feature usage through Firebase Analytics
- **Online Status**: Presence information (online/offline status)

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties

- **Firebase Services**: Analytics, crash reports, and performance monitoring
- **Supabase Backend**: Authentication and data storage services

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

### 2.1 Core Services
- Creating and managing your account
- Authenticating users via OTP
- Displaying your profile to potential matches
- Facilitating match requests and connections
- Enabling real-time messaging between matched users
- Providing search and filter functionality

### 2.2 Safety and Security
- Verifying user profiles (admin verification)
- Implementing privacy protections (photo blur, female profile protection)
- Processing block and report requests
- Preventing fraud and abuse
- Enforcing our Terms of Service

### 2.3 Communication
- Sending push notifications for matches, messages, and requests
- Providing customer support
- Sending important updates about the service

### 2.4 Improvement and Analytics
- Analyzing app usage to improve features
- Monitoring app performance and crashes
- Understanding user preferences and behavior
- Developing new features

## 3. Privacy Features

### 3.1 Female Profile Protection
- Female profiles are hidden by default and only visible to approved connections
- Female users have enhanced privacy controls
- Profile visibility is request-based, not public browsing

### 3.2 Photo Privacy
- Photos are automatically blurred until a connection is established
- Users control who can view their unblurred photos
- Photos are securely stored and encrypted

### 3.3 Request-Based Matching
- No unsolicited contact allowed
- All interactions require mutual consent
- Users can accept or reject match requests

## 4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

### 4.1 With Other Users
We share your profile information with:
- Users you match with (after mutual acceptance)
- Users who send you match requests (limited information)
- Users searching with filters matching your profile (subject to privacy settings)

### 4.2 With Service Providers
We share data with trusted third parties:
- **Supabase**: Backend infrastructure, database, authentication, storage
- **Firebase**: Analytics, crash reporting, push notifications
- **Cloud Storage**: Secure photo storage

### 4.3 Legal Requirements
We may disclose information if required by:
- Legal obligations or court orders
- Protecting rights, property, or safety
- Investigating fraud or security issues
- Enforcing our Terms of Service

### 4.4 We DO NOT:
- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your data for advertising purposes
- Publicly display your profile without consent
- Share your contact information without permission

## 5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

- **Encryption**: All sensitive data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- **Row Level Security (RLS)**: Database-level access control
- **Secure Authentication**: OTP-based phone verification
- **Input Validation**: Protection against SQL injection and attacks
- **Secure Storage**: Photos stored in encrypted cloud storage
- **Access Controls**: Limited employee access to user data

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

### 6.1 Access and Control
You can:
- View and edit your profile information
- Update your preferences and settings
- Control photo visibility
- Manage match requests
- Block or report users

### 6.2 Data Deletion
You can request:
- Account deletion (removes all personal data)
- Photo deletion (removes specific photos)
- Chat history deletion
- Profile deactivation (temporary)

To delete your account, contact us at support@nikahbook.com

### 6.3 Communication Preferences
You can control:
- Push notification settings
- Match request notifications
- Message notifications
- Marketing communications (if any)

## 7. Data Retention

- **Active Accounts**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Accounts**: Personal data deleted within 30 days
- **Legal Requirements**: Some data retained for legal compliance
- **Backup Systems**: Data removed from backups within 90 days
- **Chat History**: Retained until manually deleted by users

## 8. Children's Privacy

NikahBook is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under 18. If we discover that a minor has provided personal information, we will delete it immediately.
